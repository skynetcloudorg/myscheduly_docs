# Contacting Support

## Official Support Channel
To ensure the fastest and most secure service, MyScheduly handles all support requests exclusively via email.

**Email Address:** `support@myscheduly.com`

## Email Template
To help us solve your problem quickly, please copy, paste, and fill out the template below in your email.

---

**Subject:** [Issue Summary] - [Your Business Name]

**Body:**

1.  **My Account Email:** (The email you use to log in)
2.  **What is happening?**
    *   *Describe the issue clearly. What were you trying to do? What happened instead?*
3.  **Where is it happening?**
    *   *Which page or tab are you on? (e.g., Team Settings, Calendar, Checkout)*
4.  **Example ID (if applicable):**
    *   *Provide an Appointment ID, Customer Name, or Voucher Code related to the issue.*
5.  **Screenshots or Video:**
    *   *Please attach a screenshot of the error message or a short video showing the behavior.*

---

## What Happens Next?
1.  **Receipt:** You will receive an automated confirmation that we have received your request.
2.  **Review:** Our support team will review your account status and the details provided.
3.  **Resolution:** We will reply directly to your email with a solution or follow-up questions.
