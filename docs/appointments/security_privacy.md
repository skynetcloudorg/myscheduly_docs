# Security and Privacy

Last updated: 2025-11-27

## Data Protection Measures
- Appointments are transmitted over secure connections.
- Access to financial details is restricted to authorized roles.
- System checks reduce double-booking and protect staff calendars.

## What Information Is Stored
- Appointment date/time, service, add-ons, and staff.
- Customer name, email, and phone number.
- Applied promo codes and totals.
- Notes you add for service quality.

## How Long Data Is Kept
- Appointment records remain part of your team’s history unless removed by your organization’s policy.
- Contact support if you need guidance on data retention.

## Your Control Over Personal Data
- Update customer information anytime under Customers.
- Request deletion of specific records through support when needed.
- Use cancellations and status updates to reflect real-world changes.
